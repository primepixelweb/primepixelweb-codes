Cancellation & Refund Policy

Effective Date: 20 May 2026

1. CANCELLATION BY CLIENT

  • Advance Payments: The 50% advance payment serves as a commitment fee and is strictly non-refundable once strategic planning, resource allocation, or development has commenced.
  • Project Withdrawal: If a client cancels after the development phase has started, they may be liable for additional costs based on the percentage of work completed.

2. SUBSCRIPTION CANCELLATION (SaaS/Maintenance)

  • Clients on monthly maintenance or "Smart Rent" plans may cancel at any time.
  • A written notice via Email or WhatsApp must be provided at least 7 business days before the next billing cycle.
  • No partial refunds are issued for the current billing period.

3. REFUND ELIGIBILITY

Refunds are only processed under the following exceptional circumstances:

  • If PrimePixel Web fails to initiate the project within 10 business days of receiving the advance payment and all required assets.
  • No refunds will be issued once the website is live, the code has been handed over, or the SEO campaign has been initiated.

4. FORCE MAJEURE & RESCHEDULING

The Company reserves the right to reschedule delivery timelines due to illness, equipment failure, natural disasters, or government restrictions. In such cases, the client will be notified immediately, and a new timeline will be established.
