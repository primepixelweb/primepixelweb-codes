Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

How much does website development cost in Navi Mumbai?
At PrimePixel Web, we offer transparent pricing. Our foundational Google setups start at ₹5,999, standard informational business websites at ₹19,999, and comprehensive E-commerce/Custom platforms starting from ₹44,999.
How long does it take to build a website?
For standard business websites, our typical delivery timeline is 3 to 7 working days. Custom E-commerce setups or highly complex platforms may take 2 to 4 weeks depending on the required functionalities and client feedback speed.
Can I pay in installments?
Yes, we offer milestone-based payment structures. Typically, we require a 50% advance to start the project, and the remaining 50% is due upon completion and before the final live launch.
Do I own the website after payment?
Absolutely. Once the project is completed and fully paid, you own 100% of the website, its design, and its content. There are no hidden licensing fees locking you in.
Can you redesign old websites?
Yes, we can revamp and upgrade your existing website. We analyze your current platform's performance and give it a premium, modern, and highly convertible redesign while preserving your existing SEO value.
What if I don't have a domain?
No problem at all. We will assist you in selecting and registering the most optimal and brandable domain name for your business.
Do you provide SEO services?
Yes, all our developed websites include basic on-page SEO. We also offer dedicated Monthly SEO services focusing on technical structure, keyword ranking, and local map optimization specifically targeting Mumbai and Maharashtra.
Do you offer e-commerce website development?
Absolutely. Our Prime Launch Elite package is designed specifically for online stores, integrating secure payment gateways like Razorpay, inventory management, and automated client notifications via SMS/WhatsApp.
Do you provide website maintenance?
Yes, we highly recommend our maintenance plans starting at ₹1,499/month. These plans cover hosting, monthly backups, security checks, and content updates, ensuring your digital platform runs flawlessly.
Can you help with Google Business Profile optimization?
Yes, optimizing Google Business Profiles is one of our core specialties. We help local businesses in Navi Mumbai fix location pins, optimize service listings, and set up automated review collection systems to boost local visibility.
